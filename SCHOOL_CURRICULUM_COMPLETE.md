### **2. School Curriculum API** ✅
**File:** `jan-studio/backend/school_curriculum_api.py`

**Endpoints:**
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/lessons` - Get lessons (with filters)
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/modules` - Get all modules
- `POST /api/school-curriculum/curricula` - Create curriculum
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/curricula` - List curricula
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/curricula/{id}` - Get curriculum details
- `POST /api/school-curriculum/progress` - Update student progress
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/progress/{student_id}` - Get student progress
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/curricula/{id}/analytics` - Get curriculum analytics
- `GET /api/school-curriculum/stats` - Get overall statistics
