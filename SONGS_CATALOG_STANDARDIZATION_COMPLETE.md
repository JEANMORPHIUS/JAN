### 2. Suno Prompt Generation

#### CODED Framework Applied
All songs now have standardized Suno prompts using the CODED framework:

- **[CONTEXT]** - Setting and atmosphere
- **[OBJECTIVES]** - What the music should achieve
- **[DETAILS]** - Specific musical elements (instruments, vocals, mood, production)
- **[EXAMPLES]** - Style references (lo-fi soul, warmth, space, emotional truth)
- **[DIRECTION]** - Final guidance and constraints

#### Prompt Structure
```

[DIRECTION] Avoid over-produced pop sheen, sterile digital sound, generic arrangements. Maintain authentic emotional core.
```
