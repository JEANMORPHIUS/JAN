## SCRIPT NOTES

### Voice-Over Delivery
- **Pace:** Conversational, not rushed. Natural pauses.
- **Tone:** Warm, protective, authentic. Older-brother energy.
- **Emphasis:** 
  - "graft" (real work)
  - "heart first" (philosophy)
  - "amplified by AI" (technology as tool)
- **London Accent:** Natural, not forced. Real, not performative.

### Key Phrases to Land
1. "It lives in the gaps. In the hands. In the graft."
2. "Real work doesn't pause for perfect conditions."
3. "The art is still yours. The craft is still yours."
4. "This is bigger than sea moss."
5. "Made by heart. Amplified by AI."

### Audio Mixing Priorities
- Voice-over always clear and present (-6dB to -9dB)
- Ambient sounds support, never compete
- Digital chimes subtle and warm, not harsh
- Lo-fi oceanic base provides texture without distraction
- Real-world sounds (bus, workshop) add authenticity

### Visual Transitions
- All transitions should feel organic, not jarring
- Warm color palette (#fbe5b2) maintained throughout
- Handheld footage maintains authentic feel
- AI animations feel integrated, not separate
- Return to product at end reinforces craft focus
