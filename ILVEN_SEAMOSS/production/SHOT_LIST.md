## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Camera Requirements
- iPhone (handheld for authentic feel)
- External mic for audio quality
- Gimbal for smooth bus shots (optional)

### Lighting
- Natural light preferred
- Warm highlights (#fbe5b2) in post
- Consistent across all segments

### Audio Requirements
- Separate audio recording for VO
- Ambient sounds on location
- Lo-fi oceanic soundtrack base
- Digital chimes for AI segments
- Clear voice-over mixing

### Post-Production
- Color grade to warm palette
- Smooth transitions between real and animated
- Depth of field effects
- Particle effects for AI segment
- Audio layering and mixing
