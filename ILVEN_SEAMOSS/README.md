This project creates a 90-second vertical video that tells the story of artisanal sea moss production, amplified by AI tools while maintaining the authentic "heart" of hand-crafted work. Grounded in London urban realism, the narrative follows an "older-brother energy" voice through the journey from local craft to global vision.

## CORE PRINCIPLES

### Operational Command
- **Prioritise Authenticity:** "Film what is felt, not just what is seen"
- **Maintain Rhythm:** Build truth through "London hum" and digital chimes
- **Heart First:** Every element serves the craft
- **Urban Realism:** Ground in real-world context

### Visual Philosophy
- Mix handheld iPhone footage with poetic AI-animated overlays
- Warm highlights (#fbe5b2) for color consistency
- Cinematic depth of field, soft particles, glowing transitions

### Audio Philosophy
- Layer lo-fi oceanic soundtrack with real-world sounds
- Bus bells, kettle simmers, London traffic
- Balance between organic and digital sounds
