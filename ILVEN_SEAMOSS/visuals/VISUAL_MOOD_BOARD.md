# Visual Mood Board: Listen İlven

**Role:** Claude — The Visual Designer  
**Aesthetic:** Handheld authenticity meets poetic AI enhancement  
**Philosophy:** Real craft, amplified by digital tools

---

## COLOR PALETTE

### Primary Palette
- **Warm Highlight:** #fbe5b2 (RGB: 251, 229, 178)
  - Use for: Color grading, warm highlights, AI glow effects
  - Application: Consistent across real footage and AI-generated content

### Supporting Colors
- **Sea Moss Green:** #4a7c59 (Natural, organic, earthy)
- **Deep Ocean:** #2c4a3e (Depth, tradition, grounding)
- **Warm Cream:** #f5e6d3 (Softness, warmth, craft)
- **London Grey:** #6b6b6b (Urban, real, grounded)
- **Accent Gold:** #d4a574 (Premium, warmth, light)

### Color Application
- **Real Footage:** Grade to warm highlights, maintain natural tones
- **AI Animations:** Use warm palette, avoid cold/blue tones
- **Text Overlays:** High contrast, readable, warm-toned backgrounds
- **Transitions:** Soft, warm glows, not harsh cuts

---

## VISUAL STYLE REFERENCES

### Documentary Realism
**Reference Style:** Handheld, authentic, imperfect
- **Inspiration:** "Salt, Fat, Acid, Heat" documentary style
- **Camera Movement:** Slight shake, natural, not overly stabilized
- **Framing:** Off-center sometimes, real moments, not staged
- **Focus:** Shallow depth of field, focus pulls, natural bokeh

### Poetic Enhancement
**Reference Style:** Soft particles, glowing light, organic motion
- **Inspiration:** Studio Ghibli environmental effects
- **Particles:** Soft, warm-toned, atmospheric (not harsh)
- **Light Effects:** Glowing bursts, warm halos, soft diffusion
- **Motion:** Organic, fluid, like breathing (not mechanical)

### Urban London Context
**Reference Style:** Real, gritty, beautiful in its authenticity
- **Inspiration:** "Fleabag" London aesthetic, "This Country" realism
- **Bus Interior:** Natural lighting, reflections, real textures
- **Streets:** Moving, dynamic, not postcard-perfect
- **Workshop:** Lived-in, real tools, authentic space

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Tagline)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, readable
- **Weight:** Medium to Bold
- **Character:** Warm, approachable, not corporate
- **Examples:** Inter, Poppins, or custom handwritten touch
- **Usage:** "Made by heart. Amplified by AI." tagline

### Secondary Font (End Card)
- **Style:** Brand name, distinctive
- **Weight:** Bold
- **Character:** Memorable, authentic
- **Usage:** "Listen İlven" branding

### Text Overlay Guidelines
- **Size:** Minimum 48pt (readable on mobile)
- **Contrast:** High contrast against background
- **Position:** Center 80% safe zone
- **Animation:** Fade in, gentle, not jarring

---

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Vertical Format (1080x1920)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep important content in center 80%
- **Rule of Thirds:** Apply naturally, don't force
- **Negative Space:** Use breathing room, don't overcrowd
- **Focus Points:** Product, hands, narrator, cities (map)

### Depth of Field
- **Macro Shots:** Very shallow, focus on texture
- **Medium Shots:** Moderate depth, subject clear
- **Wide Shots:** Deeper focus, establish environment
- **AI Overlays:** Soft focus, dreamlike, integrated

### Framing Principles
- **Handheld:** Natural, slightly off-center acceptable
- **Product Shots:** Centered, hero treatment
- **Narrator:** Eye-level, direct connection
- **Bus Shots:** Through window, reflections add texture

## VISUAL ELEMENTS

### Sea Moss Product
**Visual Treatment:**
- **Texture:** Macro emphasis, show detail, natural beauty
- **Color:** Rich greens, natural variation, not uniform
- **Lighting:** Warm, side-lit for depth, not flat
- **Container:** Simple, clean, let product be hero
- **Packaging:** Minimalist, hand-crafted feel

### Workshop Environment
**Visual Treatment:**
- **Authenticity:** Real tools, lived-in space, not sterile
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, warm tones
- **Details:** Show process, not just result
- **Texture:** Wood, metal, water, organic materials
- **Atmosphere:** Cozy, craft-focused, human-scale

### London Bus Context
**Visual Treatment:**
- **Interior:** Natural lighting, real textures, reflections
- **Exterior:** Moving streets, dynamic, urban energy
- **Route:** Show signage if possible (329, Wood Green, Enfield Lock)
- **Moment:** Authentic, not staged, real commute
- **Atmosphere:** Hustle, planning, dreams in motion

### AI Tool Icons (Segment 4)
**Visual Treatment:**
- **Style:** Minimalist, recognizable, warm-toned
- **Animation:** Orbit product, organic motion, "friendly fireflies"
- **Glow:** Warm (#fbe5b2), soft, not harsh
- **Size:** Small enough to not overwhelm product
- **Motion:** Gentle, breathing-like, not mechanical
- **Icons Needed:**
  1. Gemini (Google AI)
  2. Claude (Anthropic)
  3. ChatGPT (OpenAI)
  4. NotebookLM (Google)
  5. Google AI Studio
  6. Nano Banana (social tool)

### Global Map (Segment 5)
**Visual Treatment:**
- **Style:** Clean, modern, warm-toned
- **Cities:** London, Paris, Lagos, Toronto, Istanbul
- **Lighting Effect:** Cities "light up" with warm glow (#fbe5b2)
- **Connection:** Subtle lines between cities (optional, don't clutter)
- **Animation:** Smooth, sequential, building momentum
- **Color:** Warm earth tones, not cold blue

## ANIMATION STYLE

### Transitions
- **Type:** Soft, organic, particle-based
- **Speed:** Gentle, not rushed
- **Effect:** Warm glows, soft fades, not hard cuts
- **Timing:** Match narrative rhythm, not mechanical

### Particle Effects
- **Style:** Soft, atmospheric, warm-toned
- **Density:** Light, not overwhelming
- **Motion:** Organic, floating, like dust in light
- **Color:** Warm highlights (#fbe5b2), not cold

### Glow Effects
- **Type:** Soft halos, warm bursts
- **Intensity:** Subtle, enhance not distract
- **Color:** Warm palette throughout
- **Application:** AI icons, city lights, product highlights

### Depth Effects
- **Type:** Cinematic depth of field
- **Focus Pulls:** Natural, not forced
- **Bokeh:** Soft, warm, organic
- **Application:** Macro shots, product focus, transitions
