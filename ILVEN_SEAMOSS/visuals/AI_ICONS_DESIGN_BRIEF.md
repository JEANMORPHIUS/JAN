# AI Tool Icons Design Brief: Segment 4 Animation

**Purpose:** Create six minimalist, recognizable icons that orbit the sea moss product like "friendly fireflies"  
**Duration:** 10 seconds of animation  
**Style:** Warm, organic, integrated with craft aesthetic

---

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### Overall Style
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, clean, warm-toned
- **Character:** Friendly, approachable, not cold or corporate
- **Integration:** Feel like part of the craft, not separate tech
- **Recognition:** Instantly recognizable as their respective AI tools

### Color Palette
- **Base:** Warm tones, consistent with #fbe5b2 palette
- **Accent:** Tool-specific colors (if applicable), but warmed
- **Glow:** Soft warm glow (#fbe5b2), not harsh
- **Background:** Transparent PNG for overlay

### Size & Scale
- **Relative Size:** Small enough to not overwhelm product
- **Proportion:** Icons should be roughly equal size
- **Scalability:** Vector or high-res PNG (minimum 512x512px)

### Animation Notes
- **Motion:** Orbit product in organic, "firefly" pattern
- **Speed:** Gentle, not rushed, breathing-like
- **Glow:** Pulsing, warm, soft
- **Entry:** Fade in smoothly, not pop in
- **Exit:** Fade out smoothly for transition

## ANIMATION PATTERN

### Orbit Path
- **Pattern:** Organic, not perfect circle
- **Distance:** Far enough to not crowd product, close enough to feel connected
- **Variation:** Each icon at slightly different distance/angle
- **Motion:** Gentle floating, like fireflies

### Timing
- **Entry:** Icons appear sequentially (0.5-1 second apart)
- **Orbit:** Continuous gentle motion for ~8 seconds
- **Exit:** Fade out together or sequentially for transition

### Glow Effect
- **Type:** Soft, warm halo around each icon
- **Intensity:** Subtle pulsing, not constant
- **Color:** Warm (#fbe5b2), consistent
- **Size:** Slightly larger than icon itself

### Particle Trail (Optional)
- **Style:** Soft, warm particles following icons
- **Density:** Light, not overwhelming
- **Motion:** Gentle, organic, like dust in light
- **Color:** Warm highlights

---

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### File Format
- **Primary:** PNG with transparency
- **Backup:** SVG (vector) if possible
- **Resolution:** Minimum 512x512px, scalable to 1024x1024px
- **Background:** Transparent

### Export Settings
- **Color Space:** RGB
- **Bit Depth:** 8-bit or 16-bit (for better color)
- **Compression:** Lossless or high quality
- **Naming:** `ILVEN_ICON_[TOOLNAME]_v01.png`

### Animation Requirements
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (match video)
- **Duration:** 10 seconds (300 frames)
- **Loop:** Smooth, seamless orbit
- **Easing:** Organic, not linear

---

## INTEGRATION WITH PRODUCT

### Positioning
- **Product:** Centered in frame
- **Icons:** Orbit around product, not overlapping
- **Depth:** Icons feel slightly above product (subtle shadow/glow)
- **Balance:** Visual balance, not crowded

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Product:** Always hero, never overshadowed
- **Icons:** Supporting cast, enhance not distract
- **Glow:** Subtle, warm, integrated
- **Overall:** Feels like one cohesive scene

### Color Harmony
- **Product:** Natural sea moss greens
- **Icons:** Warm-toned, complementary
- **Glow:** Warm highlights (#fbe5b2)
- **Background:** Clean, simple, product-focused
