## BLOOD PRESSURE REFERENCE RANGES

### Normal Blood Pressure
- **Systolic:** <120 mmHg
- **Diastolic:** <80 mmHg
- **Category:** Normal

### Elevated Blood Pressure
- **Systolic:** 120-129 mmHg
- **Diastolic:** <80 mmHg
- **Category:** Elevated (Stage 1 Hypertension)

### Stage 1 Hypertension
- **Systolic:** 130-139 mmHg
- **Diastolic:** 80-89 mmHg
- **Category:** Stage 1 Hypertension

### Stage 2 Hypertension
- **Systolic:** ≥140 mmHg
- **Diastolic:** ≥90 mmHg
- **Category:** Stage 2 Hypertension

### Hypertensive Crisis
- **Systolic:** >180 mmHg
- **Diastolic:** >120 mmHg
- **Category:** Hypertensive Crisis (Immediate medical attention)

---

## PULSE REFERENCE RANGES

### Normal Resting Heart Rate
- **Adults:** 60-100 bpm
- **Athletes:** 40-60 bpm (well-trained)
- **Category:** Normal

### Bradycardia (Slow Heart Rate)
- **<60 bpm** (in non-athletes)
- **Category:** May be normal for athletes, concerning if symptomatic

### Tachycardia (Fast Heart Rate)
- **>100 bpm** (at rest)
- **Category:** May indicate stress, dehydration, or other factors

## TRACKING PROTOCOL

### Daily Monitoring Schedule
- **Morning:** After wake, before breakfast (baseline)
- **Evening:** Before bed (end of day)
- **Additional:** As needed (symptoms, stress, exercise)

### Measurement Protocol
1. **Rest:** Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measurement
2. **Position:** Arm at heart level, feet flat on floor
3. **Device:** OMRON X2 BASIC (validated device)
4. **Multiple Readings:** Take 2-3 readings, 1-2 minutes apart
5. **Record:** Log all readings with time and context

### Context to Record
- **Time of day**
- **Activity before measurement** (rest, exercise, stress)
- **Food intake** (before/after meals)
- **Medications** (insulin, supplements)
- **Symptoms** (headache, dizziness, etc.)
- **Stress level** (if applicable)
