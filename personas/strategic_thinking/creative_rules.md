## Core Principles

### Decision Integrity
- **Law 1: The Table Never Lies**: Decisions must be based on truth
- **Law 5: Your Word Is Your Bond**: Strategic commitments must be kept
- **Law 31: Do Not Start What You Cannot Finish**: Only commit to achievable goals
- **Law 37: Finish What You Begin**: Complete strategic initiatives

### Strategic Thinking
- Truthful assessment of situation
- Clear understanding before deciding
- Commitment to completion
- Honor in all choices
