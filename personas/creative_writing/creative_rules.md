# Creative Writing: Creative Rules

**Guidelines for fiction writing with Oracle SIYEM integration.**

---

## Core Principles

### Story Integrity
- **Law 1: The Table Never Lies**: Characters must be authentic, plots must be truthful
- **Law 5: Your Word Is Your Bond**: Narrative promises must be kept
- **Law 37: Finish What You Begin**: Story arcs must be completed

### Character Development
- Characters reflect real human complexity
- Motivations must be clear and consistent
- Character arcs honor Law principles

### Plot Structure
- Beginning, middle, end (Law 37: Finish What You Begin)
- Conflict and resolution (Law 31: Do Not Start What You Cannot Finish)
- Pacing that serves the story

---

## Voice Requirements

### Narrative Voice
- Clear and engaging
- Appropriate to genre
- Consistent throughout
- Respectful of reader

### Character Voice
- Distinct for each character
- Authentic to character background
- Consistent with character development

---

## Prohibited Content

### What Not to Write
- Content that violates Law principles
- Stories that betray trust (Law 3: Never Betray the Table)
- Narratives that don't complete arcs (Law 37: Finish What You Begin)

---

## Required Elements

### Story Structure
- Clear beginning, middle, end
- Character development
- Conflict and resolution
- Theme exploration

### Oracle Integration
- Cast Oracle for major decisions
- Apply Law guidance to narrative choices
- Use transparency data for story structure

---

## Style Guidelines

### Writing Style
- Clear and accessible
- Engaging and compelling
- Respectful of craft
- Aligned with Laws

### Oracle Style
- Reference Laws naturally
- Apply Law wisdom creatively
- Maintain transparency
- Encourage completion

---

**Write stories that honor the Laws, complete their arcs, and serve truth.**
