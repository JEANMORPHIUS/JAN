## PART 3: THE REPLACEMENT SYSTEM - RESTORATIVE TRUTH

### Structure:

#### 1. **COMMUNITY JUSTICE COUNCILS**

**Instead of:** Courts with judges and prosecutors

**We Create:** Community Justice Councils

**Structure:**
- 7-13 community members (rotating, diverse)
- Trained in restorative justice principles
- Not professional judges, but community representatives
- Consensus-based decisions (not adversarial)

**Process:**
1. **Truth Circle:** Person shares what happened (no lawyers, no defense, just truth)
2. **Community Listens:** Council listens without judgment, seeks to understand
3. **Root Cause Exploration:** Why did this happen? What brokenness led here?
4. **Restoration Plan:** How do we heal? What amends are needed?
5. **Community Support:** How does community support healing and reintegration?

**Outcome:** Restoration, not punishment. Healing, not exclusion.

#### 2. **TRUTH AND RECONCILIATION PROCESS**

**Instead of:** Trials and sentencing

**We Create:** Truth and Reconciliation Process

**Steps:**
1. **Acknowledge the Truth:** What happened? (from all perspectives)
2. **Understand the Root:** Why did this happen? (brokenness, not criminality)
3. **Hear the Harm:** Who was harmed? How? (from victims/community)
4. **Make Amends:** What restoration is needed? (not punishment, but healing)
5. **Heal Together:** How do we move forward? (reintegration, not exclusion)

**Focus:** Truth, not winning. Healing, not punishment. Community, not system.

#### 3. **ACCOUNTABILITY MIRRORS**

**Instead of:** External punishment (jail, fines, criminal record)

**We Create:** Accountability Mirrors

**The Mirror System:**
- Regular self-reflection sessions (daily/weekly)
- Honest assessment of actions and impacts
- Community check-ins (not surveillance, but support)
- Progress tracking (healing journey, not compliance)

**The Truth:**
- You can't fool yourself in the mirror
- The mirror reflects truth
- Accountability to self is more powerful than accountability to system

#### 4. **RESTORATION CONTRACTS**

**Instead of:** Legal sentences and criminal records

**We Create:** Restoration Contracts

**Components:**
1. **Acknowledgment:** I acknowledge what happened and the harm caused
2. **Understanding:** I understand why this happened (root cause)
3. **Amends:** I commit to these specific actions to make amends
4. **Healing:** I commit to healing the root brokenness (therapy, support, etc.)
5. **Community:** I commit to community reintegration (not isolation)

**Duration:** Based on healing, not time. When restoration is complete, contract is complete.

**Record:** No criminal record. Only restoration record (for community healing, not future punishment).
