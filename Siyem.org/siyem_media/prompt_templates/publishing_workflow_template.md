### Galactic Philosophy: The Four Forms

**Galaxies come in different forms, each shaped by gravity, time, and cosmic interactions. Just as galaxies evolve in different ways, so do people and communities. Our publishing workflows must honor all these paths.**

**The Four Forms:**
- **Spiral (Active):** Rotating arms, active star formation - rapid growth, dynamic engagement. Publishing for active content, rapid distribution, dynamic workflows.
- **Barred Spiral (Structured):** Central bar channels energy - structured paths, clear navigation. Publishing for structured workflows, clear progression, linear processes.
- **Elliptical (Legacy):** Old stars, little gas - legacy wisdom, mentorship markers. Publishing for legacy archives, contemplative depth, low-gas high-wisdom features.
- **Irregular (Transformation):** No defined shape, highly active - transformation, flexible journeys. Publishing for transformation projects, flexible workflows, adaptive systems.

**Together, these forms reveal the many paths miracles take as they evolve over lifetimes.**

**This entity's publishing workflows honor all four forms. Distribution can serve Spiral (active content), Barred Spiral (structured workflows), Elliptical (legacy archives), or Irregular (transformation projects) journeys.**

## Publishing Levels

### Level 1: Internal Publishing
- **Scope**: Publishing within ecosystem only
- **Approval**: Station admin approval required
- **Audit**: Standard audit logging
- **Restrictions**: No external distribution

### Level 2: Limited External Publishing
- **Scope**: Limited external distribution
- **Approval**: Siyem.org approval required
- **Audit**: Enhanced audit logging
- **Restrictions**: Limited distribution channels

### Level 3: Full External Publishing
- **Scope**: Full external distribution
- **Approval**: Siyem.org super-admin approval required
- **Audit**: Comprehensive audit logging
- **Restrictions**: Full distribution with monitoring
