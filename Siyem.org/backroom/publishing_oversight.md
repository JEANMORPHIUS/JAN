# Publishing Oversight: Content Publishing and Distribution Control

## Purpose
Defines publishing oversight, content distribution controls, and publishing approval processes for all content published through the JAN MUHARREM ecosystem.

## Publishing Authority

### Publishing Hierarchy
- **Siyem.org Final Authority**: Siyem.org has final authority on all publishing decisions
- **Station Publishing Authority**: Stations can publish within their scope with approval
- **Approval Requirements**: Publishing requires appropriate approval level
- **Override Authority**: Siyem.org can override any publishing decision

### Publishing Levels

#### Level 1: Internal Publishing
- **Scope**: Publishing within ecosystem only
- **Approval**: Station admin approval required
- **Audit**: Standard audit logging
- **Restrictions**: No external distribution

#### Level 2: Limited External Publishing
- **Scope**: Limited external distribution
- **Approval**: Siyem.org approval required
- **Audit**: Enhanced audit logging
- **Restrictions**: Limited distribution channels

#### Level 3: Full External Publishing
- **Scope**: Full external distribution
- **Approval**: Siyem.org super-admin approval required
- **Audit**: Comprehensive audit logging
- **Restrictions**: Full distribution with monitoring

## Publishing Workflow

### Pre-Publication Review
1. **Content Review**: Review content for quality and compliance
2. **License Verification**: Verify licensing status
3. **Rights Clearance**: Clear all necessary rights
4. **Policy Compliance**: Ensure policy compliance
5. **Approval Process**: Obtain required approvals
6. **Publication Preparation**: Prepare content for publication

### Publication Process
1. **Publication Request**: Submit publication request
2. **Review and Approval**: Review and approve publication
3. **Distribution Setup**: Set up distribution channels
4. **Publication Execution**: Execute publication
5. **Confirmation**: Confirm successful publication
6. **Monitoring**: Begin post-publication monitoring

### Post-Publication Monitoring
1. **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor publication performance
2. **Compliance Monitoring**: Monitor compliance with terms
3. **Feedback Collection**: Collect and review feedback
4. **Issue Management**: Manage any issues that arise
5. **Reporting**: Generate publication reports
