# Content Protection: Intellectual Property and Digital Rights Management

## Purpose
Defines content protection mechanisms, intellectual property protection, watermarking, and digital rights management for all creative outputs within the JAN MUHARREM ecosystem.

## Content Categories

### Protected Content Types
- **Lyrics**: Song lyrics (Turkish and English)
- **Music**: Musical compositions and recordings
- **Text Content**: Written content (stories, articles, wisdom)
- **Visual Content**: Images, graphics, visual art
- **Audio Content**: Audio recordings, audiobooks
- **Video Content**: Video productions
- **Multimedia**: Combined media content

### Content Classification

#### Public Content
- **Public Domain**: Content intended for public use
- **Open License**: Content with open licenses
- **Limited Protection**: Basic protection measures

#### Internal Content
- **Internal Use Only**: Content for internal use
- **Standard Protection**: Standard protection measures
- **Access Control**: Access control required

#### Confidential Content
- **Restricted Access**: Restricted access only
- **Enhanced Protection**: Enhanced protection measures
- **Strict Access Control**: Strict access control

#### Secret Content
- **Highest Security**: Highest security classification
- **Maximum Protection**: Maximum protection measures
- **Super-Admin Access**: Super-admin access only

## Watermarking

### Watermark Types

#### Visible Watermarks
- **Entity Watermark**: Entity-specific visible watermark
- **Copyright Notice**: Copyright notice watermark
- **Brand Watermark**: Brand/logo watermark
- **Usage**: Public-facing content, previews

#### Invisible Watermarks
- **Digital Fingerprint**: Unique digital fingerprint
- **Metadata Watermark**: Embedded metadata
- **Steganographic Watermark**: Hidden in content
- **Usage**: All published content

### Watermark Implementation

#### Text Content Watermarking
- **Character-Level**: Character-level watermarking
- **Word-Level**: Word-level watermarking
- **Paragraph-Level**: Paragraph-level watermarking
- **Metadata Embedding**: Metadata embedding

#### Audio Content Watermarking
- **Frequency Domain**: Frequency domain watermarking
- **Time Domain**: Time domain watermarking
- **Perceptual Watermarking**: Perceptual audio watermarking
- **Metadata Embedding**: Audio metadata embedding

#### Visual Content Watermarking
- **Pixel-Level**: Pixel-level watermarking
- **DCT Watermarking**: Discrete cosine transform
- **Wavelet Watermarking**: Wavelet transform watermarking
- **Metadata Embedding**: EXIF/IPTC metadata

### Watermark Requirements
- **Tamper Resistance**: Resistant to tampering
- **Robustness**: Robust against common transformations
- **Invisibility**: Invisible watermarks not perceptible
- **Uniqueness**: Unique watermark per content instance
- **Traceability**: Traceable to source and user
