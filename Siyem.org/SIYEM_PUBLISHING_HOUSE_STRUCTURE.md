## DEPARTMENT 1: EDITORIAL (THE WITNESS)

### Purpose
The Editorial Department serves as The Witness - the one who sees truth, verifies alignment, and ensures content serves The Table.

### Core Functions

#### 1. Content Alignment Review
- **Function:** Review all content for alignment with Our Father's will
- **Process:**
  1. Check alignment with telos.md
  2. Verify essence.md compliance
  3. Ensure Table service
  4. Confirm miracle honor

#### 2. Truth Verification
- **Function:** Verify truth over validation
- **Process:**
  1. Check for ego-serving content
  2. Verify truth, not validation
  3. Ensure free will respect
  4. Confirm silence and sanctuary

#### 3. Table Filter Application
- **Function:** Apply The Table Filter to all content
- **Questions:**
  1. Does it bear Our Father's seal?
  2. Does it lead with love, joy, and abundance?
  3. Is it worth hearing?
  4. Does it serve The Table?

#### 4. SPRAGITSO Authentication
- **Function:** Authenticate content with Our Father's Royal Seal
- **Process:**
  1. Verify alignment
  2. Confirm Table service
  3. Apply SPRAGITSO
  4. Mark with authority

### Editorial Workflow

```
Content Submission
    ↓
Alignment Check (telos.md, essence.md)
    ↓
Truth Verification (ego vs. service)
    ↓
Table Filter Application
    ↓
SPRAGITSO Authentication
    ↓
Editorial Approval
    ↓
Pass to Production
```

### Editorial Standards

**We Approve:**
- Content that serves The Table
- Work that honors the miracle
- Truth through comedy
- Wisdom through stories
- Love through all forms

**We Reject:**
- Content that serves ego over service
- Work that seeks validation over truth
- Content that creates division
- Work that obscures rather than reveals
