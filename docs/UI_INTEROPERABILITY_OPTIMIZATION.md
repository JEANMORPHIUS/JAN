## DESIGN SYSTEMS

### Easy on the Eyes (Light Mode)

**Color Scheme:**
- Background: Soft light gray (#f8f9fa) - Not pure white
- Text: Soft dark gray (#212529) - Not pure black
- Accents: Soft blue, green, yellow
- Borders: Very light (#dee2e6)
- **Purpose:** Reduce eye strain, comfortable reading

**Typography:**
- Font Size: 16px base (comfortable reading)
- Line Height: 1.6 (comfortable spacing)
- Font Family: System fonts (native, optimized)
- **Purpose:** Easy reading, no eye strain

**Spacing:**
- Comfortable padding and margins
- Consistent spacing scale
- **Purpose:** Visual breathing room

**Shadows:**
- Subtle shadows
- Soft depth
- **Purpose:** Gentle elevation, not harsh

**Transitions:**
- Smooth transitions (150ms-500ms)
- Ease-in-out curves
- **Purpose:** Natural feel, not jarring

---

### Easy on the Eyes (Dark Mode)

**Color Scheme:**
- Background: Soft dark (#1a1a1a) - Not pure black
- Text: Soft light (#e9ecef) - Not pure white
- Accents: Soft blue, green, yellow
- Borders: Soft (#3d3d3d)
- **Purpose:** Reduce eye strain in dark mode
