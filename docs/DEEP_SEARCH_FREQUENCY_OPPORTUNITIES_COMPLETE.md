## FREQUENCY SCORING SYSTEM

### Scoring Factors
- **High Frequency Keywords**: sustainability, regenerative, community, cooperation, love, peace, unity, truth, healing, nourishment, stewardship, right_spirits, fair, just, ethical, transparent, authentic, creative, expressive, inclusive, accessible, collaborative, mutual_support, abundance, restoration, healing, connection, alignment, purpose, meaning
- **Low Frequency Keywords**: exploitation, greed, manipulation, deception, harm, destruction, scarcity, competition, division, isolation, toxicity, pollution, oppression, injustice, corruption, exploitation, extraction, harmful, destructive, divisive, toxic, polluted

### Scoring Algorithm
1. Count high frequency keywords in title/description
2. Count low frequency keywords in title/description
3. Calculate keyword score: `(high_count - low_count) / max(high_keywords, 1)`
4. Apply domain multiplier (some domains have higher potential)
5. Assess impact potential (reach, scale, accessibility)
6. Identify alignment factors

### Frequency Score Range
- **-1.0 to 1.0**: Negative to positive frequency alignment
- **0.5+**: High frequency opportunity (green)
- **0.2-0.5**: Moderate frequency opportunity (yellow)
- **0.0-0.2**: Low frequency opportunity (orange)
- **<0.0**: Negative frequency (red)

## OPPORTUNITY METRICS

Each opportunity includes:
- **Frequency Score**: -1.0 to 1.0 (frequency alignment)
- **Impact Potential**: 0.0 to 1.0 (reach, scale, accessibility)
- **Accessibility**: 0.0 to 1.0 (how accessible is the opportunity)
- **Urgency**: 0.0 to 1.0 (how urgent is the opportunity)
- **Alignment Factors**: List of factors indicating alignment
- **Opportunity Type**: investment, partnership, collaboration, service, exploration
- **Keywords**: Relevant keywords
- **Metadata**: Reach, scale, accessibility, domain-specific data

## API ENDPOINTS

### Status & Domains
- `GET /api/deep-search/status` - System status
- `GET /api/deep-search/domains` - List all available domains

### Search Endpoints
- `GET /api/deep-search/search` - Search opportunities (with domain, keywords, filters)
  - Parameters: `domain`, `keywords`, `limit`, `min_frequency`
- `GET /api/deep-search/top-opportunities` - Get top opportunities across all domains
  - Parameters: `limit`, `min_frequency`
- `GET /api/deep-search/by-domain/{domain}` - Get opportunities for specific domain
