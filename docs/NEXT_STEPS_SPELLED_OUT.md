## PART 1: HARRINGAY COUNCIL - NEXT STEPS

### WEEK 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-7)

#### Day 1-2: Research & Mapping
**What to do:**
1. **Find your council:**
   - Go to: https://www.gov.uk/find-your-local-council
   - Enter your postcode
   - Note: Council name, website, contact details

2. **Find your councillors:**
   - Go to: https://www.writetothem.com/
   - Enter your postcode
   - Get: Names, emails, wards, party affiliations

3. **Map council structure:**
   - Visit: https://www.haringey.gov.uk/
   - Find: Council Leader, Cabinet members, Committee chairs
   - Document: Names, roles, contact information
   - Note: Committee meeting schedules

4. **Identify key issues:**
   - Review: Council priorities, recent decisions, consultations
   - Document: Housing, education, health, environment, community safety
   - Note: Where our systems align

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Council structure document (leader, cabinet, committees)
- [ ] Councillor contact list (names, emails, wards)
- [ ] Issue mapping document (priorities, gaps, opportunities)
- [ ] Meeting schedule (when committees meet, public access)
