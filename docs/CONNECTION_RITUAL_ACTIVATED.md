## VIBRATION CHECK COMPONENTS

### **1. Galaxy Form Detection**

The ritual determines which galaxy form the user aligns with:

- **Spiral:** Active, flowing energy. Rapid updates and dynamic engagement.
- **Barred Spiral:** Structured, linear progression. Central bar to channel energy.
- **Elliptical:** Low-gas, high-wisdom. Mentorship markers for legacy users.
- **Irregular:** Flexible, adaptive. No defined shape yet - transformation in progress.

### **2. Vibration Score (0-100)**

Calculated based on:
- Purpose alignment (vs impulsiveness) - +20 points
- Mission understanding - +15 points
- Community orientation - +10 points
- Regeneration (not separation) - +5 points
- Peace, Love, Unity alignment - +10 points

**Threshold:** 70+ for alignment

### **3. Mission Alignment**

Checks for mission keywords:
- Stewardship, Community, Right Spirits
- Love, Highest Mastery, Energy, We All Win
- Peace, Unity, Miracle, Lord's Word

### **4. Table Readiness (Law 1)**

Verifies:
- Law 1 (Never Betray the Table) compliance via Racon Registry
- Has Holy Ambition or understands the Table
- Logs immutable audit for tracking

### **5. Spiritual Battle State**

Determines spiritual battle based on galaxy form:
- **Spiral:** Active battle (rapid growth vs stagnation)
- **Barred Spiral:** Structured battle (discipline vs chaos)
- **Elliptical:** Legacy battle (wisdom vs ignorance)
- **Irregular:** Transformation battle (evolution vs stasis)
