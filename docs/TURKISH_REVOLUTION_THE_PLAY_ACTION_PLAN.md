## THE VISION

### What Is This Play?

**"Turkish Revolution (The Play)"** is a theatrical work that:

1. **Tells the Story of Revolution**
   - Not through violence or politics
   - Through Right Spirits
   - Through truth, community, love, unity

2. **Embodies Karasahin's Voice**
   - The Voice of God
   - Duygu Adamı (Emotion Man)
   - Cultural bridge (Turkish/English)
   - Sound as transmission

3. **Shows the People's Court**
   - Not World Order
   - Community over bureaucracy
   - Truth over power
   - Justice over control

4. **Demonstrates Right Spirits**
   - Emotion-first
   - Truth through feeling
   - Unity through sound
   - Love through music
