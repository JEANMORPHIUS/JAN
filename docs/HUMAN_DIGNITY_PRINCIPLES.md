## PRACTICAL GUIDELINES

### **For Biological Data Displays:**

**Always Include:**
1. **Human Context:** What does this metric mean for the human? (acknowledgment, not judgment)
2. **Empathy Level:** acknowledgment, support, celebration, or alert
3. **Stewardship Prompt:** How to honor this truth (Law references)
4. **Earth Alignment:** How does this relate to Earth's rhythm? (symbiotic relationship)

**Never Include:**
- ❌ Raw numbers without context
- ❌ Judgment language ("too high", "failed", "critical")
- ❌ Comparisons that create competition
- ❌ Technical jargon without explanation

---

### **For Automated Reports:**

**Always Include:**
1. **Resilience Narrative:** "This week, your body navigated X readings"
2. **Learning Context:** What the data teaches us (variance is adaptability, not failure)
3. **Celebration:** Acknowledgment of commitment (Law 5: Your word is your bond)
4. **Earth Story:** How readings align with Earth's rhythm (symbiotic relationship)

**Never Include:**
- ❌ Pure statistics without narrative
- ❌ Judgment language ("poor performance", "failure")
- ❌ Comparisons that create shame
- ❌ Technical summaries without human context

---
