## HOW TO START

### As an Individual:

**Daily Practice:**
1. Look in the mirror
2. Ask yourself:
   - Was I truthful today?
   - Did I hide my vulnerabilities?
   - What am I avoiding looking at?
3. Write down reflections
4. Own your actions
5. Make amends where needed

**Use API:**
```bash
POST /mirror/reflect
# Track your daily reflections
# Monitor your growth
# Practice truth-telling
```

### As a Community:

**Form a Council:**
1. Recruit 7-13 community members
2. Complete restorative justice training (4 weeks)
3. Hold first Truth Circle
4. Create Restoration Contract
5. Track success stories

**Use API:**
```bash

POST /council/{council_id}/truth-circle
```
