## Time Banking Details

### How It Works
- One hour of work = one time credit
- All labor valued equally (doctor = childcare = gardening)
- Members offer services, earn credits, spend credits
- No money needed, builds community

### Services Exchanged
- Care work (childcare, eldercare, disability support)
- Home repair and maintenance
- Gardening and food production
- Teaching and tutoring
- Transportation and errands
- Professional services (legal, medical, accounting)

### Benefits
- Values all work equally (dignity)
- Recession-proof (no money shortage)
- Builds community relationships
- Accessible to all (no wealth barrier)
- Complements cash economy

### Examples
- **Dane County TimeBank** (Wisconsin): 30 years, 1,000+ members
- **Tempo Time Credits** (UK): 300+ locations
- **hOurworld**: Software platform for time banks globally

## Local Currency Details

### How It Works
- Community issues currency (paper or digital)
- Accepted by local businesses
- Convertible to dollars at set rate (or not)
- Encourages local spending (local multiplier effect)

### Benefits
- Money circulates locally (3-7x multiplier vs. dollars)
- Recession-proof (community controls supply)
- Supports local businesses
- Builds community identity
- Reduces dependence on national economy

### Examples
- **Ithaca Hours** (NY): 25+ years, 900+ businesses
- **BerkShares** (MA): $2.5 million in circulation
- **Bristol Pound** (UK): First city-wide digital currency
- **Chiemgauer** (Germany): 3 million euros in circulation

## Islamic Finance Principles

### No Interest (Riba)
- Interest is exploitation (earning from time, not work)
- Profit-sharing instead (risk shared equally)
- Ethical investing (no harm industries)

### Models
- **Mudarabah**: Profit-sharing partnership
- **Musharakah**: Joint venture (shared ownership)
- **Murabaha**: Cost-plus financing (transparent markup, not interest)
- **Qard Hassan**: Benevolent loan (no interest, repay principal only)

### Application
- Mortgages: Bank buys house, sells to buyer at higher price (transparent, fixed)
- Business loans: Profit-sharing instead of interest
- Consumer goods: Cost-plus or lease-to-own

### Results
- Ethical finance ($3 trillion industry)
- Shared risk (not debtor exploitation)
- Real economy focus (not speculation)
