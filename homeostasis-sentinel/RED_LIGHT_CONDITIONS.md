# RED LIGHT CONDITIONS: Protocol Stop Criteria

**This document defines the absolute "RED LIGHT" conditions that require immediate protocol termination, regardless of code recommendations.**

---

## CRITICAL RED LIGHT CONDITIONS

### 1. Vision Clarity < 2
**Condition:** Current vision clarity drops to or below 2/10
**Action:** **STOP PROTOCOL IMMEDIATELY**
**Rationale:** Represents risk of permanent vascular or neurological damage. Vision below this threshold indicates severe osmotic pressure or metabolic crisis.

### 2. Predicted Vision < 2 (Forecast)
**Condition:** TrendForecaster predicts vision clarity < 2 within next 12 hours
**Action:** **STOP PROTOCOL IMMEDIATELY**
**Rationale:** Prevents reaching critical threshold. System breach detection.

### 3. Unconsciousness or Severe Disorientation
**Condition:** Any loss of consciousness, severe confusion, or inability to track time/place
**Action:** **STOP PROTOCOL IMMEDIATELY + SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION**
**Rationale:** Indicates severe metabolic crisis (potentially DKA). Medical emergency.

### 4. Persistent Vomiting or Inability to Hydrate
**Condition:** Unable to keep water/fluids down for >2 hours
**Action:** **STOP PROTOCOL + SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION**
**Rationale:** Risk of severe dehydration and electrolyte imbalance. Cannot maintain basic hydration.

### 5. Severe Chest Pain or Cardiac Symptoms
**Condition:** Chest pain, irregular heartbeat, severe dizziness, or fainting
**Action:** **STOP PROTOCOL + SEEK IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION**
**Rationale:** Potential cardiac complications. Medical emergency.

## YELLOW LIGHT CONDITIONS (Prepare to Stop)

### 1. Vision Clarity < 3 for >4 hours
**Condition:** Vision remains below 3/10 for more than 4 consecutive hours despite intervention
**Action:** **PREPARE TO STOP. Monitor every 30 minutes.**
**Rationale:** Approaching critical threshold. If no improvement, proceed to RED LIGHT.

### 2. Muscle Tension > 9 for >6 hours
**Condition:** Muscle tension remains above 9/10 for more than 6 hours despite flush/clearance
**Action:** **PREPARE TO STOP. Escalate intervention.**
**Rationale:** Extreme tension indicates severe metabolic stress. System may be unable to clear acidity.

### 3. Acidosis Risk: 5+ Consecutive Days
**Condition:** High tension (>7) + low vision (<5) for 5+ consecutive days
**Action:** **PREPARE TO STOP. Consider protocol pause.**
**Rationale:** Persistent acidosis risk indicates protocol may not be sustainable at current parameters.
