## Morning Protocol (Go/No-Go)

### 1. Calibration (Zero Point)
**Upon Waking:**
- Check vision clarity (1-10 scale)
- Check blood glucose (finger prick)
- Record in Obsidian with timestamp
- **This is your "Zero Point" baseline**

**Decision Point:**
- If vision < 4 BEFORE first Loop → **YELLOW LIGHT** (consider postponing)
- If vision 4-6 → Proceed with extreme caution
- If vision > 6 → Proceed as planned

### 2. First Sun (Circadian Sync)
- First Obsidian entry = moment you see the sun
- This establishes your circadian baseline
- Record `circadian_sync_score` (estimate)

### 3. The First Loop
- Record first urine re-integration
- Monitor vision closely for next 30 minutes
- **If vision blurs within 30 minutes** → Loop is too "heavy" today
- **Action**: 2-hour Water-Only window before next loop

---

## Critical Monitoring Points

### Vision Velocity
- Monitor for rapid drops (e.g., 8 → 5 in 2 hours)
- Rapid decline = Warning regardless of average

### Double-Down Rule
- If Glucose >180 AND Vision <4 → **STOP LOOP**
- Critical osmotic pressure detected
- Action: 1L Alkaline Water + 20 Min Swim

### Recovery Rate
- After swim/sauna: Check vision improvement within 60 minutes
- If no improvement → Mechanical lever failed
- Action: Water Flush or medical intervention

### Break Glass Condition
- If Glucose >250 AND Vision <3 → **PROTOCOL FAILING**
- Loop theory not matching physical reality
- Have fast-acting insulin ready

## Action Protocols (Mutually Exclusive)

### IF Acidosis Detected:
- **Action**: STOP LOOP. DRINK 1L ALKALINE WATER. 20 MIN SWIM.
- Avoid Loop re-integration until tension drops below 5

### IF Osmotic Pressure Detected:
- **Action**: SAUNA 15 MIN. NO WATER FOR 30 MIN. MONITOR VISION.
- High loop frequency with declining vision requires clearance

### IF Double-Down (High Glucose + Low Vision):
- **Action**: STOP LOOP. DRINK 1L ALKALINE WATER. 20 MIN SWIM.
- Critical osmotic pressure - Loop must be paused

## Red Light Conditions (Absolute Stop)

1. **Vision Clarity < 2** (current or predicted)
2. **Break Glass**: Glucose >250 AND Vision <3
3. **Unconsciousness/Severe Disorientation**
4. **Persistent Vomiting** (unable to hydrate)
5. **Cardiac Symptoms** (chest pain, irregular heartbeat)
