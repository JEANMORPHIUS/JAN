## The Three Critical Sensors

### 1. The Acetone "Crossing"
- **Metric:** `breath_quality` < 3 (Acetone/Metallic)
- **Meaning:** Blood becoming acidic (overcharging battery)
- **Action:** Monitor closely. If combined with tension >8 = CURRENT ACIDOSIS
- **Danger:** The "Big C" transition point - most dangerous moment

### 2. The Hydrostatic Lever (20 MIN SWIM)
- **Visualization:** Water pressure pushing "syrupy" glucose out of extremities → kidneys
- **Threshold:** Vision must improve by +1.0 point within 60 minutes
- **If Failed:** Osmotic pull > hydrostatic push = Water Flush required
- **Cue:** Sugar concentration too high for mechanical clearance alone

### 3. The Emotional "Noise" (Cortisol)
- **Tag in Obsidian:** `#crises` for high-stress moments
- **Effect:** High cortisol → spikes glucose → triggers Velocity Warning
- **Distinction:** Adrenal (emotional) vs Metabolic (physical)
- **Action:** Use journal tags to correlate vision drops with emotional state

## Critical Alerts (Priority Order)

1. **Hard-Deck Kill-Switch** → Vision <2 predicted → STOP PROTOCOL

5. **Velocity & Glycogen Paradox** → Low glucose + declining vision → Crash risk
