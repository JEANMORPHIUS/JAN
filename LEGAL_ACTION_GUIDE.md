### 2. **OMBUDSMAN COMPLAINT** ‚öñÔ∏è
**YES - This is the right next step:**

#### What is an Ombudsman?
- Independent oversight body
- Investigates complaints about public services (including police)
- Can force police to act
- Free service

#### How to File:
1. **Find your local Ombudsman:**
   - UK: Police and Crime Commissioner (PCC) or Independent Office for Police Conduct (IOPC)
   - US: Civilian Complaint Review Board (CCRB) or Police Ombudsman
   - Other countries: Search "[Country] police ombudsman"

2. **File Complaint:**
   - Online form (fastest)
   - Phone call
   - Written letter
   - Include all documentation

3. **What to Include:**
   - Your original police report number
   - Dates of harassment
   - Evidence you've collected
   - Timeline of police inaction
   - Why you believe they're stalling

**Timeline:** Usually responds within 2-4 weeks
