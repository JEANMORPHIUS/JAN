# Edible Cyprus Relationship Documentation

**Partnership with Edible Cyprus**  
**Relationship:** Buyer/Distributor (Edible London) ↔ Supplier (Edible Cyprus)

---

## THE RELATIONSHIP

### Simple Basis
- **Edible Cyprus:** Grows the food in Cyprus
- **Edible London:** Sells/distributes the food to communities

### Partnership Model
- **Edible Cyprus:** Production partner (supplier)
- **Edible London:** Distribution partner (buyer/distributor)
- **Relationship:** Supplier-buyer partnership
- **Alignment:** Shared mission and values

## OPERATIONAL COORDINATION

### Order Process
1. **Edible London:** Places order based on distribution needs
2. **Edible Cyprus:** Confirms production capacity
3. **Coordination:** Agrees on quantity, timing, delivery
4. **Production:** Edible Cyprus grows/harvests
5. **Shipping:** Transport from Cyprus to UK
6. **Delivery:** Receipt at Edible London (20-22 Bernard Road, N15 4NE)
7. **Distribution:** Edible London distributes to communities

- **Route:** Cyprus → UK
