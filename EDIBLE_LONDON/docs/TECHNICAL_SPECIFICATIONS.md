## VIDEO FORMAT

### Primary Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio:** Vertical (9:16)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 pixels
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Codec:** H.264 or H.265
- **Bitrate:** 8-12 Mbps (for upload quality)
- **Audio:** AAC, 48kHz, stereo

### Platform Optimization
Optimized for:
- **Instagram Reels**
- **TikTok**
- **YouTube Shorts**
- **Facebook Reels**

### Alternative Exports (if needed)
- **Horizontal (16:9):** 1920x1080 for YouTube/Twitter
- **Square (1:1):** 1080x1080 for Instagram feed
- **4K Vertical:** 2160x3840 for future-proofing (if source allows)

## AUDIO SPECIFICATIONS

### Layered Sound Design

#### Base Layer
- **Music:** Lo-fi oceanic soundtrack
- **Mood:** Calming, rhythmic, understated
- **Volume:** -12dB to -18dB (background layer)
- **License:** Ensure proper licensing for commercial use

#### Real-World Sounds
- **Bus Bells:** London bus arrival/departure sounds
- **Kettle Simmers:** Boiling water, pouring tea
- **London Traffic:** Ambient city sounds
- **City Ambience:** Street conversations, movement
- **Volume:** -6dB to -12dB (mid layer)

#### Digital Layer
- **AI Tool Chimes:** Subtle digital sounds for tool icons
- **Transitions:** Soft whooshes, gentle chimes
- **Volume:** -9dB to -15dB (subtle layer)
- **Style:** Organic digital (not harsh or robotic)

#### Voice-Over
- **Recording:** Professional quality, clear audio
- **Volume:** -3dB to -6dB (primary layer)
- **Style:** Warm, conversational London accent
- **Processing:** Light compression, gentle EQ

### Audio Mix Priorities
1. **Voice-Over:** Always clear and prominent
2. **Music:** Sets mood without overwhelming
3. **Real-World Sounds:** Adds authenticity
4. **Digital Sounds:** Subtle enhancement

### Final Mix
- **LUFS:** -16 LUFS (platform optimized)
- **Peak Level:** -1dB (headroom for platform compression)
- **Stereo Width:** Maintained throughout

## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Camera Settings (iPhone)
- **Resolution:** 4K if possible, downscaled to 1080p
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (match delivery format)
- **Stabilization:** Use built-in stabilization or gimbal
- **Focus:** Manual focus for product shots (macro)
- **Exposure:** Slightly underexpose for color grading flexibility

### Color Grading Workflow
1. Import footage
2. Apply warm highlight tone (#fbe5b2)
3. Match real footage to AI-generated clips
4. Ensure consistency across all segments
5. Final review for brand aesthetic

### Animation Workflow
1. Design tool icons (6 total)
2. Create orbital animation path
3. Add glow and particle effects
4. Match timing to music beats
5. Composite with real product footage
