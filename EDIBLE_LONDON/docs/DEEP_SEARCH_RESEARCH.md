### Mission & Core Activities

**Primary Mission:**
Edible London aims to **redefine how food is produced, shared, and used** while enhancing community resilience.

**Core Focus Areas:**

1. **Growing Food**
   - Regenerating derelict or underused urban land into community growing spaces
   - Creating green, accessible hubs for connection with nature, learning, and community activities
   - Transforming grey urban spaces into edible gardens

2. **Preventing Food Waste**
   - Collecting surplus fruit and vegetables that would otherwise go to waste
   - Sorting and redirecting high-quality produce to food banks and cook-ups
   - Composting unsuitable material or sending to anaerobic digesters

3. **Fostering Resilience**
   - Supporting people with skills and support: youth engagement, counselling, mentoring, volunteering, education
   - Building resilient communities beyond just food distribution

**Business Sectors:**
- Growing vegetables
- Wholesale of fruit & vegetables
- General food services
- Educational services

---

### Impact & Scale

**Food Distribution:**
- Since 2020: Distributed ingredients equivalent to **over 1 million meals** to those facing food insecurity
- During COVID-19 lockdown: Scaled up significantly
- Peak week: Distributed **50,000 meals worth of ingredients** in a single week

**Waste Diversion:**
- Saved **hundreds of tonnes** of surplus fruit & veg from landfill

**Volunteer Engagement:**
- **Over 350 volunteers** registered
- Activities include: growing, surplus food sorting, community outreach, delivering surplus food, zero-waste creative projects

**Staffing:**
- Began as volunteer-led
- Now has paid staff including:
