## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project creates a 90-second vertical video that tells the story of artisanal production, amplified by AI tools while maintaining the authentic "heart" of hand-crafted work. Grounded in London urban realism, the narrative follows an "older-brother energy" voice through the journey from local craft to global vision.

This blueprint provides a comprehensive project-level instruction for an AI creative agent, designed to manage a build that blends artisanal craft with digital amplification. This version removes specific "Siyem Media" branding to focus on the universal "Independent Creator" workflow.

## RELATIONSHIP TO EDIBLE CYPRUS

**The Basis is Simple:**
- **Edible Cyprus:** Grows the food in Cyprus
- **Edible London:** Sells/distributes the food to communities

**Partnership Model:**
- Edible Cyprus is the **production partner** (supplier)
- Edible London is the **distribution partner** (buyer/distributor)
- Together, they create a complete supply chain aligned with the mission

**Shared Mission:**
- Redefine how food is produced, shared, and used
- Enhance community resilience
- Build sustainable food systems

**Connection:**
- Edible Cyprus grows food in Cyprus → Supplies to Edible London
- Edible London receives supply → Distributes to North London communities
- Combined impact: Production + Distribution = Complete cycle
