## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Camera Requirements
- iPhone (or equivalent mobile camera)
- Stabilizer/gimbal for smooth movement
- Macro lens attachment (for product detail)
- Good lighting (natural preferred, supplement as needed)

### Audio Requirements
- Voice-over: Professional recording setup
- Ambient sounds: Record on location
- Music: Pre-selected, licensed track
- Sound effects: Library or recorded

### Color Grading Notes
- Warm highlights (#fbe5b2) throughout
- Consistent palette across all segments
- Match real footage to AI-generated clips
- Maintain artisanal, warm aesthetic

### Timing Notes
- Total duration: ~90 seconds
- Pacing: Rhythm should match London "hum" and audio
- Transitions: Smooth, natural flow
- Key moments: Allow space for impact
