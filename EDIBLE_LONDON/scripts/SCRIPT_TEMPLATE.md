## SCRIPT DEVELOPMENT GUIDELINES

### Voice Characteristics
- **Older-brother energy:** Supportive, knows what's up
- **Warm London banter:** Friendly, conversational
- **Everyday philosopher:** Observes deeply, speaks simply
- **Authentic:** Real talk, no corporate speak

### Writing Principles
- ✅ Write like talking to a friend
- ✅ Use specific London references (329 bus, Wood Green, Enfield Lock)
- ✅ Make philosophical points feel natural
- ✅ Balance humor with sincerity
- ✅ Show the "graft" and real work
- ✅ Reinforce "heart first, AI second"

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Corporate jargon
- ❌ Over-explaining
- ❌ Hyperbole or false excitement
- ❌ Making technology sound mystical
- ❌ Generic "inspirational" language
- ❌ Forgetting the craft focus
