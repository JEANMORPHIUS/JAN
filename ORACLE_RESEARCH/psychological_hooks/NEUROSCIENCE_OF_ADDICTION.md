## THE DOPAMINE SYSTEM

### HOW IT WORKS:

**Normal Dopamine Release:**
- Eating: +50% dopamine

- Social connection: +50% dopamine

**Gambling Dopamine Release:**
- Near-miss: +200% dopamine (higher than winning!)

- Creates compulsive behavior
