## THE RELATIONSHIP

### Simple Basis
- **Edible Cyprus:** Grows the food in Cyprus
- **Edible London:** Sells/distributes the food to communities

### Complete Supply Chain
```
CYPRUS PRODUCTION → UK DISTRIBUTION → COMMUNITY IMPACT
```

## SHARED MISSION

### Edible London's Mission
"Redefine how food is produced, shared, and used while enhancing community resilience."

### Edible Cyprus Contribution
- **Produces** food sustainably in Cyprus
- **Supplies** food to Edible London
- **Enables** Edible London's distribution mission
- **Supports** community resilience through production

### Combined Impact
Together, they create a complete cycle:
1. **Production** (Cyprus) → Sustainable growing
2. **Supply** (Cyprus to UK) → Reliable supply chain
3. **Distribution** (UK) → Community access
4. **Impact** (Communities) → Food security and resilience
