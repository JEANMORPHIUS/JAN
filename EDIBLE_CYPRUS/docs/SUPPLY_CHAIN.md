# Supply Chain: Edible Cyprus → Edible London

**Supply Chain Documentation**  
**Flow:** Cyprus Production → UK Distribution

---

## THE SUPPLY CHAIN MODEL

### Simple Basis
- **Edible Cyprus:** Grows the food in Cyprus
- **Edible London:** Sells/distributes the food to communities

### Complete Flow
```

┌─────────────┐         ┌──────────────┐      ┌─────────────┐
│             │         │              │      │             │

   Production              Distribution           Impact
```

## SUPPLY CHAIN STAGES

### Stage 1: Production (Cyprus)
**Location:** Cyprus  
**Organization:** Edible Cyprus  
**Activity:** Growing food

**Responsibilities:**
- Sustainable agricultural practices
- Quality control during growing
- Harvesting and preparation
- Quality assurance before shipping

**Output:** High-quality produce ready for distribution

---

### Stage 2: Supply (Cyprus → UK)
**Route:** Cyprus to UK  
**Activity:** Logistics and shipping

**Responsibilities:**
- Coordination between Edible Cyprus and Edible London
- Shipping and logistics management
- Quality maintenance during transport
- Delivery to Edible London facilities

**Output:** Produce delivered to Edible London

---

### Stage 3: Distribution (UK)
**Location:** North London, UK  
**Organization:** Edible London  
**Activity:** Distribution to communities

**Responsibilities:**
- Receiving and processing supply
- Distribution to food banks
- Cook-ups and community meals
- Volunteer coordination (350+ volunteers)
- Surplus management

**Output:** Food distributed to communities

---

### Stage 4: Impact (Communities)
**Location:** North London communities  
**Activity:** Community access and impact

**Impact:**
- Food security for communities
- 1M+ meals distributed (ingredients)
- Community resilience building
- Education and support programs

**Outcome:** Enhanced community resilience

## ORDER MANAGEMENT

### Order Process
1. **Edible London:** Places order based on distribution needs
2. **Edible Cyprus:** Confirms production capacity
3. **Coordination:** Agrees on quantity, timing, delivery
4. **Production:** Edible Cyprus grows/harvests
5. **Shipping:** Transport from Cyprus to UK
6. **Delivery:** Receipt at Edible London
7. **Distribution:** Edible London distributes to communities

### Communication
- **Primary Contact:** partnerships@ediblelondon.org
- **Coordination:** Regular communication on orders
- **Updates:** Production status, shipping updates
- **Feedback:** Quality and impact feedback
