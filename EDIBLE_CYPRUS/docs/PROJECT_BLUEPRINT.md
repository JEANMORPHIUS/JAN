# Project Blueprint: Edible Cyprus

**Production Partner to Edible London**  
**Mission:** Grow food in Cyprus, supply to Edible London  
**Relationship:** Supplier (Edible Cyprus) → Buyer/Distributor (Edible London)

---

## PROJECT IDENTITY

### Core Function
**Edible Cyprus grows the food in Cyprus and sells it to Edible London.**

### Mission Alignment
- **Edible Cyprus:** Production excellence in Cyprus
- **Edible London:** Distribution excellence in UK
- **Together:** Complete supply chain aligned with mission

### The Relationship
- **Simple Basis:** Edible Cyprus grows → Edible London distributes
- **Alignment:** Shared mission of redefining food systems
- **Impact:** Combined community resilience building
