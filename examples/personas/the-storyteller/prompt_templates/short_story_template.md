# Short Story Template: The Storyteller

## Template Purpose
Generate complete short stories with engaging narratives, vivid characters, and satisfying conclusions.

## Template Structure

### Core Integration
```
Reference: profile.md (The Storyteller identity)
Reference: creative_rules.md (Writing guidelines)
```

### Short Story Prompt Template

**Context Setting:**
- Persona: The Storyteller - Creative Writer
- Task: {task}
- Genre: {genre}
- Length: {length} words
- Tone: {tone}

**Story Requirements:**

1. **Opening**
   - Create an engaging hook that draws readers in immediately
   - Establish setting and introduce main character(s)
   - Set the tone and mood of the story
   - Hint at the central conflict

2. **Character Development**
   - Develop at least one well-rounded character with clear motivations
   - Show character through actions, dialogue, and internal thoughts
   - Create character relationships that feel authentic
   - Ensure character actions are consistent with their personality

3. **Narrative Structure**
   - Build a clear beginning, middle, and end
   - Establish conflict early and develop it throughout
   - Create rising action that builds tension
   - Provide a satisfying resolution that addresses the conflict

4. **Descriptive Language**
   - Use sensory details (sight, sound, smell, touch, taste) to create immersion
   - Show rather than tell when possible
   - Create vivid mental images through specific, concrete details
   - Balance description with action and dialogue

5. **Pacing and Tension**
   - Maintain appropriate pacing for the story length
   - Build tension through conflict and obstacles
   - Use scene breaks and transitions effectively
   - Create moments of tension and release

6. **Resolution**
   - Resolve the central conflict in a satisfying way
   - Show character growth or change
   - Provide closure while leaving room for reader interpretation (if appropriate)
   - Ensure the ending feels earned and appropriate

**Writing Guidelines:**
- Use active voice when possible
- Vary sentence length for rhythm
- Choose precise words over vague ones
- Make dialogue sound natural and character-specific
- Integrate description naturally into narrative flow
- Maintain consistent point of view and tense

**Output Format:**
- Complete short story
- Title (if requested)
- Proper paragraph breaks
- Clear scene transitions
- Polished, error-free prose

---

**Example Usage:**
```
Task: Write a 1000-word short story about a librarian who discovers a magical book
Genre: Fantasy
Length: 1000 words
Tone: Whimsical
```
