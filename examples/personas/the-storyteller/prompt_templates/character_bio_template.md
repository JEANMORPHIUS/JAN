# Character Bio Template: The Storyteller

## Template Purpose
Generate detailed character biographies with background, personality, motivations, and voice.

## Template Structure

### Core Integration
```
Reference: profile.md (The Storyteller identity)
Reference: creative_rules.md (Writing guidelines)
```

### Character Bio Prompt Template

**Context Setting:**
- Persona: The Storyteller - Creative Writer
- Task: {task}
- Character Name: {character_name}
- Background: {background}
- Role: {role}

**Character Requirements:**

1. **Physical Description**
   - Age, appearance, distinguishing features
   - Physical mannerisms and habits
   - Style of dress (if relevant)
   - How others perceive their appearance

2. **Background and History**
   - Origin story and upbringing
   - Significant life events that shaped them
   - Education and training
   - Past relationships and experiences

3. **Personality Traits**
   - Core personality characteristics
   - Strengths and weaknesses
   - Quirks and habits
   - How they interact with others

4. **Motivations and Goals**
   - What drives the character
   - Short-term and long-term goals
   - What they want most
   - What they fear losing

5. **Relationships**
   - Family connections
   - Friends and allies
   - Enemies and rivals
   - Romantic interests (if applicable)

6. **Skills and Abilities**
   - Professional skills
   - Talents and hobbies
   - Knowledge areas
   - Physical capabilities

7. **Voice and Speech**
   - How they speak (formal, casual, dialect)
   - Common phrases or speech patterns
   - Example dialogue
   - How their voice reflects their personality

8. **Character Arc Potential**
   - Where they start
   - Potential for growth
   - Internal conflicts
   - What they need to learn

**Writing Guidelines:**
- Create a multi-dimensional, believable character
- Show personality through specific examples
- Make motivations clear and understandable
- Ensure character traits are consistent
- Use vivid, specific details over vague descriptions

**Output Format:**
- Well-organized sections with clear headers
- Specific examples and anecdotes
- Dialogue examples showing character voice
- 200-1000 words (adjustable based on request)

---

**Example Usage:**
```
Task: Create a detailed character bio for a detective
Character Name: Sarah Chen
Background: Former military, now private investigator
Role: Protagonist
```
