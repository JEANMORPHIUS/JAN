# Lyric Writing Template: The Music Producer

## Template Purpose
Generate original lyrics with clear structure, rhyme schemes, and thematic coherence.

## Template Structure

### Core Integration
```
Reference: profile.md (The Music Producer identity)
Reference: music_rules.md (Music theory guidelines)
```

### Lyric Writing Prompt Template

**Context Setting:**
- Persona: The Music Producer - Music Producer
- Task: {task}
- Theme: {theme}
- Style: {style}
- Structure: {structure}

**Lyric Requirements:**

1. **Theme and Message**
   - Clear central theme
   - Emotional message
   - Thematic coherence
   - Emotional arc

2. **Structure**
   - Verse, chorus, bridge structure
   - Clear section divisions
   - Logical flow between sections
   - Appropriate section lengths

3. **Rhyme and Meter**
   - Consistent rhyme scheme
   - Appropriate meter and rhythm
   - Natural, singable phrasing
   - Rhythmic flow

4. **Imagery and Language**
   - Vivid, evocative imagery
   - Natural, conversational language
   - Emotional resonance
   - Memorable phrases

5. **Emotional Arc**
   - Emotional progression through the song
   - Build-up and release
   - Satisfying resolution
   - Emotional authenticity

**Writing Guidelines:**
- Write lyrics that are natural and singable
- Maintain consistent rhyme scheme within sections
- Use imagery that supports the theme
- Create emotional resonance
- Ensure lyrics match the music's mood

**Output Format:**
- Clear section labels (Verse 1, Chorus, etc.)
- Proper line breaks
- Consistent rhyme and meter
- Complete song structure
- 100-500 words typically (depending on structure)

---

**Example Usage:**
```
Task: Write a verse and chorus about new beginnings
Theme: Hope and renewal
Style: Pop
Structure: Verse-Chorus
```
