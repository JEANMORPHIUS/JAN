## THE REPLACEMENT SYSTEMS

### **1. Universal Basic Energy (UBE)**

**Model:** Everyone receives free basic energy allotment

**Tiers:**
- 0-100 kWh/month: FREE (essential needs)
- 100-200 kWh/month: FREE (comfortable living)
- 200+ kWh/month: Small fee (luxury/excessive use)

**Result:** 95% of people pay $0 for electricity

### **2. Community Energy Commons**

**Model:** Energy treated as commons, not commodity

**Structure:**
- Community-owned solar/wind/geothermal
- Local control, democratic governance
- Surplus shared or stored
- No corporate monopoly

**Benefits:**
- Local resilience
- Community ownership
- Environmental sustainability
- Profit stays local

### **3. Public Utility Restoration**

**Steps:**
1. Nationalize/municipalize utilities
2. Remove profit motive
3. Free basic tier for all
4. Rapid renewable transition

**Funding:**
- Tax-funded (like roads, schools)
- Wealth tax on billionaires
- Corporate taxes
- Carbon tax on luxury use

### **4. Specific Utilities:**

| Utility | Free Replacement | Technology |
|---------|-----------------|------------|
| **Electricity** | Solar + wind + geothermal + batteries | Distributed generation, smart grid |
| **Gas/Heating** | Heat pumps + district heating + hydrogen | Electric heat pumps, geothermal, green H2 |
| **Water** | Public system, no shut-offs | Infrastructure repair, conservation incentives |
