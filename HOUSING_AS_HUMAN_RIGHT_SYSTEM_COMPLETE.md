## PART 3: THE REPLACEMENT SYSTEM - FREE HOUSING FOR ALL

### How Free Housing Works:

#### 1. **UNIVERSAL BASIC HOUSING (UBH)**

**Model:** Everyone receives adequate housing as right

**Structure:**

**Tier 1: Emergency Housing**
- **For:** Currently homeless
- **Type:** Immediate placement in existing empty homes/hotels
- **Timeline:** Within 24 hours
- **Support:** Case managers, mental health, addiction services
- **Cost:** Free, no preconditions
- **Duration:** Permanent (Housing First model)

**Tier 2: Transitional Housing**
- **For:** Unstable housing (eviction risk, couch surfing)
- **Type:** Studio/1-bedroom units
- **Timeline:** Immediate waitlist, placement within 1 month
- **Support:** Financial counseling, job training
- **Cost:** Free or income-based (0-30% income)
- **Duration:** Until stable

**Tier 3: Family Housing**
- **For:** Families with children
- **Type:** 2-4 bedroom homes/apartments
- **Timeline:** Priority placement
- **Support:** Childcare, education, health services
- **Cost:** Free or income-based
- **Quality:** Safe, clean, accessible

**Tier 4: Specialized Housing**
- **For:** Elderly, disabled, veterans, specific needs
- **Type:** Accessible units, elder-friendly, trauma-informed
- **Support:** Specialized services
- **Cost:** Free
- **Quality:** Dignity-centered

**Tier 5: Community Housing**
- **For:** Everyone else
- **Type:** Cooperative housing, co-housing, eco-villages
- **Support:** Community governance, shared resources
- **Cost:** Minimal (maintenance only)
- **Ownership:** Community land trusts

#### 2. **HOUSING FIRST PROTOCOL**

**Immediate Implementation:**

**Step 1: House Everyone Immediately**
- Identify all homeless people
- Identify all empty homes/hotels
- Match people to homes
- Move people in within 24-48 hours
- No preconditions (no sobriety requirements, no work requirements)

**Step 2: Provide Wrap-Around Support**
- Case managers assigned
- Mental health services
- Addiction treatment (if wanted)
- Job training (if wanted)
- Healthcare access
- Community connection

**Step 3: Make Housing Permanent**
- No time limits
- No arbitrary rules
- Housing is right, not privilege
- Support continues as long as needed

**Evidence:**
- Finland ended homelessness this way
- Utah reduced chronic homelessness 91%
- Cheaper than shelters/streets
- Better outcomes for everyone

#### 3. **COMMUNITY LAND TRUSTS (CLT)**

**Model:** Remove land from speculation, give to community

**Structure:**

**How It Works:**
- Non-profit trust owns land
- Community controls trust democratically
- Land leased to residents (99-year leases)
- Residents own buildings, not land
- Resale price controlled (no speculation)
- Land stays affordable forever

**Benefits:**
- Removes land from market
- No gentrification
- Permanent affordability
- Community control
- Prevents displacement
- Generational stability

**Governance:**
- 1/3 residents
- 1/3 community members
- 1/3 public representatives
- Democratic decision-making
- Transparent processes

**Examples:**
- Burlington, VT (successful 30+ years)
- UK has 300+ CLTs
- Growing globally
