## Welcome Letter

Dear Educator,

Welcome to JAN Studio! You're about to embark on an exciting journey teaching AI and creativity to your students.

**What You Have:**
Your kit includes everything you need to get started:
- Raspberry Pi 5 with JAN Studio pre-installed
- Example personas ready to use
- Complete curriculum and worksheets
- Tutorial videos
- Full documentation

**Getting Started:**
1. Plug in the power supply
2. Wait 30 seconds for the Pi to boot
3. Open http://localhost:8000 in your browser
4. Follow the Quick Start Card for your first creation

**What Your Students Will Learn:**
- How AI can be used creatively
- Digital literacy and critical thinking
- Creative writing and expression
- Responsible AI use

**Support:**
- Full documentation is on the SD card
- Tutorial videos are pre-loaded
- Email support: support@janstudio.edu
- Online help: janstudio.edu/help

**Next Steps:**
1. Read the Quick Start Guide (on SD card)
2. Watch the tutorial videos
3. Try creating your first persona
4. Explore the curriculum
5. Introduce it to your students!

**We're Here to Help:**
Don't hesitate to reach out if you have questions. We want you and your students to succeed!

Happy creating!

The JAN Studio Team
