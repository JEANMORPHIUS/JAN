## Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Needs Improvement (1) | Score |
|----------|---------------|----------|-------------------|----------------------|-------|
| **Rule Types** | Includes all three types (must-do, must-not-do, style). Rules are correctly categorized. | Includes 2-3 rule types. Most are correctly categorized. | Includes 1-2 rule types. Some categorization errors. | Missing rule types or incorrect categorization. | ___/4 |
| **Rule Quality** | All rules are specific, clear, and actionable. No vague rules. | Most rules are specific and clear. Few vague rules. | Some rules are specific. Several vague rules. | Most rules are vague or unclear. | ___/4 |
| **Rule Organization** | Rules organized by importance. Clear priority structure. | Rules mostly organized. Some priority structure. | Rules somewhat organized. Unclear priorities. | Rules not organized or no clear structure. | ___/4 |
| **Rule Effectiveness** | Rules clearly affect output. Evidence of rules working. | Rules mostly affect output. Some evidence of effectiveness. | Rules somewhat affect output. Limited evidence. | Rules don't affect output or no evidence. | ___/4 |
