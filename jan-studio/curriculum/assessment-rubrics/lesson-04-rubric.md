## Rubric

|----------|---------------|----------|-------------------|----------------------|-------|

| **Prompt Quality** | All prompts are specific, clear, and detailed. Easy to understand. | Most prompts are specific and clear. Generally understandable. | Some prompts are specific. Some are vague. | Most prompts are vague or unclear. | ___/4 |
| **Prompt Variety** | Creates 3 different types of prompts (story, explanation, creative). Shows versatility. | Creates 2-3 different types. Shows some variety. | Creates 1-2 different types. Limited variety. | Creates only one type or no variety. | ___/4 |
| **Prompt Refinement** | Clearly improves at least one prompt. Shows evidence of iteration and better results. | Improves one prompt. Some evidence of iteration. | Attempts to improve prompt. Limited evidence. | No evidence of improvement or iteration. | ___/4 |
