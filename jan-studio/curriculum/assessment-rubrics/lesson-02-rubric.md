## Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Needs Improvement (1) | Score |
|----------|---------------|----------|-------------------|----------------------|-------|
| **Identity** | Clear name and role. Purpose is well-defined and specific. | Name and role present. Purpose is somewhat clear. | Name present but role/purpose unclear. | Missing name or role. | ___/4 |
| **Description** | Detailed description (3+ sentences). Clearly explains what persona does and how. | Good description (2-3 sentences). Explains most aspects. | Basic description (1-2 sentences). Lacks detail. | Description is missing or very vague. | ___/4 |
| **Rules** | At least 3 clear, specific rules. Rules are well-organized and effective. | At least 3 rules. Most are clear and specific. | 2-3 rules. Some are vague or unclear. | Fewer than 2 rules or rules are unclear. | ___/4 |
| **Improvement** | Shows clear improvement over first persona. More detailed and better organized. | Shows some improvement. More detailed than before. | Shows minimal improvement. Slightly better than before. | No improvement or worse than first persona. | ___/4 |
