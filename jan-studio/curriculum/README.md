# JAN Studio Educational Curriculum

**Complete curriculum for teaching AI and creativity using JAN Studio**

---

## Overview

This curriculum is designed for students ages 10-16 to learn about AI, creativity, and digital literacy through hands-on experience with JAN Studio.

**Duration:** 5 lessons (45 minutes each)  
**Total Time:** 3.75 hours  
**Grade Level:** 10-16 years  
**Subject Areas:** Creative Writing, Technology, Digital Literacy

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this curriculum, students will:

1. **Understand** what AI personas are and how they work
2. **Create** effective personas with clear purposes and rules
3. **Write** effective prompts for content generation
4. **Generate** and refine creative content
5. **Present** their work and explain their process
6. **Understand** responsible AI use

## Prerequisites

### For Teachers
- Basic computer skills
- Raspberry Pi setup (see setup guide)
- JAN Studio installed and running

### For Students
- Basic reading and writing skills
- Basic computer skills (typing, using a browser)
- No prior AI knowledge required
