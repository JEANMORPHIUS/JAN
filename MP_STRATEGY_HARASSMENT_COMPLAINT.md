## WHAT TO INCLUDE IN EMAIL

### For MPs Specifically:
- **Your Full Address with Postcode:** MPs can only help constituents
- **Constituency Confirmation:** "I am a constituent in [Your Area]"
- **Request for Escalation:** "I request your assistance in escalating this matter"
- **Timeline:** Clear timeline of police inaction
- **Evidence:** All documentation attached

### MP-Specific Language:
"I am writing as your constituent to request your assistance in escalating a serious matter of police inaction on a harassment complaint I filed.

As my Member of Parliament, I believe you have the authority and relationships to:
1. Escalate this matter directly to police leadership
2. Contact the Police and Crime Commissioner on my behalf
3. Ensure this complaint receives proper investigation
4. Hold police accountable for their inaction

I trust you will take immediate action to ensure I receive the protection and justice I am entitled to."
