### HOW THE BETTING INDUSTRY WORKS

#### 1. **THE VIG (VIGORISH) - THE HOUSE EDGE**

**What It Is:**
- The built-in advantage for the bookmaker
- Typically 4-5% on single bets
- Can reach 30%+ on accumulators
- This is how they ALWAYS win long-term

**How It Works:**
```
Example: Football Match
- Team A: -110 (1.91 decimal)
- Team B: -110 (1.91 decimal)

If both sides have equal money:
- Bookmaker collects: $200
- Pays out: $191 (on winner)
- Profit: $9 (4.5% vig)

This is the house edge - built into every bet.
```

**The Truth:**
- Every bet has a vig
- Bookmakers don't need to predict outcomes
- They just need balanced action
- The vig guarantees profit
